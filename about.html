    ABOUT ME

Welcome! I'm Malyck, an adventurous Omani captivated by travel and the great outdoors. The spark ignited during an awe-inspiring hiking trip in Colorado's rocky mountains back in 2015. The following year marked a milestone as I embarked on my inaugural international escapade—a solo expedition across Iceland. Despite a tight budget, I immersed myself in Iceland's breathtaking landscapes, capturing their splendor on film while embracing unconventional accommodations.

Since then, I've ventured solo across developing nations in Asia, Central America, and the Middle East. Enriched by extraordinary encounters and dauntless hurdles, I've witnessed the Lombok earthquake in Indonesia and navigated through turbulent times like the bombings in Bangkok.

Throughout my odyssey, I've been blessed with indelible memories—slumbering in ethereal jungle treehouses in Bali, conquering the base camp of Mount Everest, and setting foot on timeless marvels such as Great Pyramids of Giza and the Taj Mahal. I continue to traverse the globe, seeking fresh exploits, and sharing captivating tales through my travel blog.
